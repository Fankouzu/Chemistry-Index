# Privacy Policy — Chemistry Index

**Last updated:** March 2026

Chemistry Index (“the App”) is an educational app for browsing and practicing junior high school chemical equations.

**In-app copy:** The same substance is available **inside the app, fully offline** (Chinese UI under “隐私政策” / shield icon). No network is required to read it. Keep this file aligned with `src/components/PrivacyPolicyPanel.tsx` when you change either text.

## Data we collect

We **do not** operate accounts, analytics servers, or advertising SDKs in the App as shipped from this repository.

## Data stored on your device

The App may store the following **only on your device** using browser or WebView storage (e.g. `localStorage`):

- **Display preferences** — such as light or dark theme.
- **Favorites** — identifiers of equations you choose to bookmark.

This data **stays on your device** unless you clear app data or uninstall the App. We do not receive this information on our servers **unless** you later integrate third-party services that change this behavior (if you fork the project, update this policy accordingly).

## Network access

The App may use network connectivity for the WebView/runtime as required by the platform. The open-source build in this repository does not require a backend for core equation browsing. If you add online features (e.g. AI APIs), you must disclose them here and in store consoles.

## Children’s privacy

The App is intended for educational use. If you believe we need to comply with specific regulations (e.g. COPPA) for your distribution, consult legal counsel and update this policy.
